Plot Summary:

Super 30 (2019) is a Hindi-language biographical drama film based on the life of Anand Kumar, a brilliant mathematician from Patna, Bihar. The story follows Anand, who gives up a prestigious job offer to dedicate his life to teaching underprivileged students for the IIT-JEE entrance exam, one of the toughest engineering exams in India. Despite limited resources and societal challenges, he trains 30 underprivileged students each year, guiding them toward academic success and a brighter future.
Hrithik Roshan as Anand Kumar – the protagonist, portraying the real-life educator with a strong sense of purpose and empathy.
